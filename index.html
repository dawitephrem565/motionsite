Validation Workshop

Validation Workshop on Integrated watershed development Plan

One day Validation work shop was organized at Ziway in hail Resort on 22nd July 2015 to evaluate and validate consultancy service on Delineation, mapping and preparation of detail management plan for micro watersheds in four wareda of Ethiopian Rift Valley lakes Basins’. Over 40 participants from Government and none governmental offices and the community member attended the workshop.

General Assembly

Annual General Assembly Meeting Held

Motions held its Annual general assembly meeting on 05/07/2015 in its office. A total of 15 participants constituted by share holders, staffs and volunteers attended the meeting. During the occasion major activities of the last fiscal year, key activity plan for the coming fiscal year were presented and deliberated.
